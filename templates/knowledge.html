What are Liquidity Pools?

Liquidity pools are like digital money markets where you can deposit your cryptocurrency and earn rewards. When you add your tokens to these pools, you're helping other people trade cryptocurrencies, and in return, you earn a portion of the trading fees. It's similar to how a bank pays you interest for depositing your money, except the returns are much higher in crypto!
