What is DeFi Yield Farming?

Yield farming is a way to earn passive income with your crypto assets. It works by providing liquidity (your tokens) to decentralized exchanges, helping other users trade more easily. In return, you earn a portion of the trading fees - this is your "yield" or "APY" (Annual Percentage Yield).
