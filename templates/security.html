Security & Audits: Non-Custodial WalletConnect on Solana

How FiLot ensures your assets remain under your control while helping you maximize yields.

FiLot Security Architecture

FiLot is designed with security as the top priority. Our non-custodial architecture means your crypto assets always remain under your control in your own wallet. FiLot never takes custody of your funds and cannot access your assets without your explicit approval for each transaction.

Non-Custodial Architecture

Your keys, your crypto, always.

FiLot uses WalletConnect v2 protocol to interact with your Solana wallet. This means:

  • Your private keys never leave your wallet
  • You approve each transaction individually
  • FiLot cannot initiate transactions without your approval
  • Connection sessions can be terminated at any time
  • Open-source WalletConnect protocol with strong encryption

Smart Contract Audits

We only recommend thoroughly vetted pools.

FiLot incorporates multiple levels of pool security verification:

  • We only support pools from audited protocols (Raydium, Orca)
  • Additional internal security review for each pool
  • Continuous monitoring for exploits or vulnerabilities
  • Immediate blacklisting of compromised pools
  • Full audit trail of pool recommendations

On-Chain APY Oracle

Transparent and verifiable yield data.

FiLot provides transparent, on-chain verification of APY data:

  • All pool APY data is derived directly from on-chain information
  • Calculations follow standard DeFi methodologies
  • Regular comparison with protocol-reported APYs
  • Full transparency in APY calculation formulas
  • Historical APY tracking for realistic projections

Enterprise-Grade Data Security

Encryption

All data transmissions are secured with TLS 1.3 encryption. Sensitive user data is encrypted at rest using AES-256.

Privacy Protection

We maintain minimal user data and follow strict privacy practices. Your investment data is never shared with third parties.

Attack Protection

Our infrastructure features DDoS protection, WAF, rate limiting, and regular penetration testing to prevent attacks.

Security Best Practices for Users

  • Only interact with FiLot through official channels
  • Use hardware wallets for significant investments
  • Verify transaction details before approving
  • Keep wallet seed phrases offline and secure
  • Enable 2FA on all related accounts
  • Disconnect your wallet when not in use

Risk Disclosure

While FiLot employs extensive security measures, DeFi investing carries inherent risks:

  • Smart Contract Risk: Despite audits, there is always a risk of undiscovered vulnerabilities in smart contracts.
  • Impermanent Loss: Price changes between paired assets can result in value reduction compared to just holding.
  • Market Risk: Cryptocurrency markets are volatile, and prices can fluctuate rapidly.
  • APY Fluctuation: Yields can change based on market conditions and are not guaranteed.

FiLot provides tools to monitor and mitigate these risks, but users should only invest funds they can afford to lose and should diversify their investments appropriately.
